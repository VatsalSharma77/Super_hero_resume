Intoduction



Yooshhh Mina-san, As You already seen my video My Name is Son-Goku also you can call me Kakarot. I am one of the survivors of the extinct Saiyan race. I was sent as a baby to planet Earth in order to destroy it. When I arrived I was a violent kid, due to my warrior nature. However, I suffered an accident which made me lose my memories. Then became a kind and calm kid. Trained by Master Yoshi, and became a talented martial artist and world's greatest defender.

Origin

Originally named Kakarrot' I was the son of Bardock, a low-class Saiyan warrior; and Gi-ne, born on Planet Vegeta in or around Age 737. Sensing the impeding danger to the Saiyan race from Frieza, my parents secretly sent me off to earth when I was only three years old. On Earth I was taken in by the elderly Son Gohan, who named me Son Goku. A difficult and violent child, I suffered head trauma in an accident and became more friendly and amenable after recovering. Grandpa Gohan trained me in martial arts, and realized that I became a bloodthirsty beast when I was exposed to the full moon. Grandpa instructed me never to look at the moon, but I disobeyed and transformed into a Great Ape. While in this transformed state, I accidentally murdered Grandpa. After the death of my adoptive grandfather, I essentially raised myself alone in the mountains.
